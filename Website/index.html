My Poems

Whispers of a Broken Heart

In the quiet night, I sit alone,
A heart so heavy, turned to stone.
The stars above, they do not shine,
For all the joy, no longer mine.
Tears fall slow, like drops of rain,
Each one filled with silent pain.
The world moves on, yet here I stay,
Lost in dreams that fade away.
The laughter’s gone, the smiles are few,
A sky once bright, now shades of blue.
Love was near, but slipped from sight,
Leaving me to chase the night.
In shadows deep, I make my bed,
With broken hopes and words unsaid.
A life once full, now torn apart,
A lonely soul, an aching heart.

The Mark of Nature

Nature leaves its mark on me,
In whispers, soft and free.
In delicate ways, it speaks so clear,
A gentle touch I hold so dear.

In the world's noise, I find my place,
By listening within, I embrace my grace.
Clarity blooms when I pause to see,
The truest self that’s hidden in me.

A complex world, but I long to find,
A peace so simple, a quiet mind.
Time moves on in endless flow,
I stay focused, letting purpose grow.

Balancing chaos with beauty’s gleam,
Life is more than it may seem.
Like an artist painting skies above,
I create new beauty, with endless love.
